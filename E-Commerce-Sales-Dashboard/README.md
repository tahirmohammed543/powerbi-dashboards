# E-Commerce Sales Performance & Profitability Dashboard (Power BI)

## Executive Summary
This Power BI dashboard provides a consolidated view of e-commerce sales performance, profitability, and customer purchasing behaviour. It enables business stakeholders to quickly identify revenue drivers, margin leakages, and regional performance gaps to support data-driven commercial decisions.

---

## Business Objectives
- Track overall sales, profit, and order trends over time  
- Identify high-performing and underperforming product categories  
- Analyse regional contribution to revenue and profitability  
- Understand customer purchasing patterns to optimise pricing and promotions  

---

## Dashboard Features & Insights
- **Trend Analysis:** Time-based visuals highlight seasonality and growth/decline patterns in sales and profit  
- **Category Performance:** Clear comparison of product categories to identify top revenue generators versus low-margin segments  
- **Regional Insights:** Region-wise breakdown helps prioritise high-growth markets and address underperforming locations  
- **Interactive Filtering:** Slicers enable quick analysis by category, region, and time period, supporting ad-hoc business questions  
- **Drill-Down Capability:** Users can move from high-level KPIs to detailed views without leaving the dashboard

## Data Model & Technical Approach
- **Star Schema Design:** Fact table for sales transactions linked to dimension tables such as Date, Product Category, and Region  
- **Relationships:** One-to-many relationships ensure accurate aggregation and filtering across visuals  
- **DAX Measures:**  
  - Calculated measures for Sales, Profit, AOV, and Order Count  
  - Use of time-intelligence functions to support trend analysis  
- **Performance Optimisation:** Measures designed to minimise redundancy and maintain report responsiveness
